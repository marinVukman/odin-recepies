Spaghetti Napoletana

Description

A light summer meal that will fill your home with smells of Tuscany!

Ingredients

  • Can of pealed tomatoes
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 large onion
  • Fresh basil and parsley
  • Olive oil
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 500g spaghetti

Steps

  1. Chop onions and garlic
  2. Stir onion and garlic in a large skillet on 3 tbs of olive oil until golden brown
  3. Add tomatoes, salt and pepper, cook at medium heat for 45 minutes stirring occasionaly
  4. Towards the end add roughly chopped basil and finely chopped parsley
  5. In salted water cook spaghetti until al dente, strain and mix with sauce
  6. serve with parmesan cheese
