Beef Soup

Description

Nothing better to warm up the chilly autumn days then this classic straight from your mothers cookbook!

Ingredients

  • 1kg of beef shoulder cut
  • Two medium sized carrots
  • Medium sized potato
  • One green bell pepper
  • One onion
  • 200g of root celery
  • 2tbs of tomato extract
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Put all ingredients into a large pot and cover with water
  2. Bring to boil, cook on medium heat for 2 hrs
  3. Remove meat, carrot and potato, cut it in small peaces
  4. Put the soup through the strainer
  5. Return the cut meat and veggies to pot and serve
