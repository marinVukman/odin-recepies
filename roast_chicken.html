Roast Chicken

Description

All the glory of the Sunday family roast with minimal hassle!

Ingredients

  • One whole chicken
  • 1kg potatoes
  • 3 large carrots
  • 1 onion

Steps

  1. Peal and chop carrots and potatoes
  2. Put carrots and potatoes in a bowl, add chilli powder, thyme, salt and olive oil and toss
  3. Rub salt on chicken skin
  4. Put the content of a bowl into a baking pan, put chicken breast down on top
  5. Bake in an oven at 180 Celsius for an hour and a half
